Tigerpaw → Rev.io PSA

Customer Migration
Marketing & Communication Plan

A coordinated, segment-driven campaign to guide all Tigerpaw legacy customers through a seamless transition to the new Rev.io PSA platform.

Foundation

What We've Been Doing

Over the past year, both Customer Success and Marketing have been laying the groundwork for this migration. While not overtly promoting the new platform, planting seeds about what was coming and how we were building product parity for what they needed.

Customer Success

  • Focus Groups with Tigerpaw customers
  • Beta & Early Access Programs
  • Client Business Reviews
  • Support ticket touchpoints highlighting Focus Groups & Beta Access

Marketing

  • Email signature campaigns introducing the new platform
  • Organic social media featuring development progress
  • Tradeshow presence and live demonstrations
  • Migration campaign live as of March 1

Segmentation

Target Audience

The campaign covers 543 accounts across two primary groups. Hosted (Unleashed) customers are the top business priority — migrating them off the legacy Unleashed platform is the primary objective. On-Prem (Tigerpaw) customers are also a high priority given ongoing hosting costs. Each group is divided into four sub-segments based on stated migration intent, with tailored outreach for each.

The "Unknown" segment across both groups requires particular care. We do not know their appetite to migrate and cannot risk inadvertently creating a reason to churn. These accounts receive a softer, newsletter-style cadence — and open/click intent data is used to gauge readiness before escalating to a direct migration ask.

Core Messaging

Four Messaging Pillars

Messaging is tailored by sub-segment and intent. All audiences, however, share four universal pillars that anchor every communication.

🚀

Platform Value

Five key differentiators — web-based access, mobile app, Revii AI agent, Community, and rapid development velocity — plus Cyber Protect and an exciting product roadmap that shows where we're going next.

✅

Product Parity

Feature-for-feature coverage for every existing Tigerpaw workflow, supported by side-by-side capability collateral. Customers can see exactly what they have today maps to in the new platform — and more.

⚡

Seamless Transition

Our new automated migration tool ensures complete data integrity and minimal disruption. Customers don't have to worry about losing what they've built — the tool handles the heavy lifting.

🤝

Rate Loyalty

We are honoring existing customer rates as a thank-you for their patience and continued partnership through the transition period. This is a tangible signal of appreciation — and a powerful retention lever.
