About This Session

The path to the boardroom is opaque — until now.

Board service is often positioned as the next logical step for senior women leaders — yet the path to that first board seat remains opaque, inconsistent, and full of unspoken rules.

This Pavilion Women Executive session cuts through the noise. Join seasoned board members who have successfully navigated — and helped reshape — the boardroom as they pull back the curtain on how women leaders actually break into board service, and how they stand out once they're there.

This is a practical, no-fluff conversation for women executives who are serious about board service — not someday, but next.

You'll walk away with clarity on…

  • How first board seats are really won — not posted
  • Making the shift from executive operator to board-level director
  • What boards actually look for — and why readiness is often misjudged
  • The unspoken rules of your first 90 days in the boardroom
  • Turning one board seat into long-term board opportunity
  • Where you stand today, how to position yourself, and what to do next
